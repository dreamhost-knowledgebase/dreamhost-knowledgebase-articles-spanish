You can preview your Remixer site before publishing it to a domain. To preview, you need to locate the Cloud ID of your site.

Previewing your site

  1. Log into your Remixer panel.
  2. On the top right, click the 'My Sites' icon.
  3. Edit your Remixer page.
  4. On the upper right toolbar you'll see a Publish or View button.
  5. If it says Publish click it to update your changes to your live site. Then click View to view your current site.

Your site is originally published to a unique cloud URL for you to preview first. When ready, you can then assign it to your website.

If you're assigning this to your actual domain name, it will take several hours for DNS to propagate online before you can see the new website.

Saving your page

Remixer automatically saves your pages. You can view all saved pages by clicking 'My Sites' on the top right. The page continues to exist until you delete it.
